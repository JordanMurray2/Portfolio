About the Game

BackGround:

You went to visit an old pyramid in ancient Egypt. You entered and now the only way out is to find your way through the pyramid maze. There are multiple hieroglyphics along the walls, but some letters are hard to make out.

How to Play:

  1. Make your way through the maze and look for the hieroglyphic clues

  2. Figure out the missing letter(s) in the puzzle to unlock the path

  3. Find your way out of the pyramid as fast as you can!
